Analysis

Since our dataset wasn't build on numbers, we had to find some ourselves. Here's some of the elements we pulled from the string and counted. We looked for more generic things, like word count and unique words, but also more online based text like emojis, links, and images. More complex things like sentiment and parts of speech tagging were pulled out of the string as well.
